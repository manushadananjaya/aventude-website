Tech Arena 2026

Aventude is participating in Tech Arena to connect with forward-thinking founders, investors, and industry leaders who are shaping the future of technology. Aventude aims to contribute to and grow further within the Nordic and global tech ecosystem

Event

Aventude Webinar

February 05th 2026

Aventude is hosting a webinar on how AI is reshaping application modernization. The session explores how organizations can move away from large, one-time transformation projects toward more incremental, OPEX-led modernization approaches enabled by AI. Designed for senior business and technology leaders, the webinar offers practical insights into building a more sustainable and adaptable modernization capability.

News

Rebuilding Education After the Dithwa Storm

February 02nd 2026

Following the devastation caused by the Dithwa storm in Sri Lanka in December 2025, Aventude employees, through the company’s ESG program, helped restore Tikiri Kumara Vidyalaya primary school to full operation by providing essential learning equipment and support.

7 Years of Innovation.

November 2025

For seven years, Aventude has been committed to delivering impactful solutions for a diverse range of clients across industries. Aventude Future Skills Workshop – Empowering Tomorrow’s Engineers

October 2025

Aventude hosted a hands-on Future Skills workshop for third-year students at the University of Moratuwa, Sri Lanka’s leading engineering university. Through practical AI driven workflows using Devin and Citrus65, students gained real-world exposure to modern software engineering approaches, reinforcing solution-oriented thinking and preparing them to build smarter, more impactful solutions

Leadership Update at Aventude

June 2025

Aventude announces a leadership transition as Co-founder Thurupathan Vijayakumar steps into the role of CEO. Co-founder Thushara Wijewardena will continue to drive Aventude’s long-term strategic growth in her role as Chairperson of the company. This transition reflects Aventude’s continued evolution and strengthens our focus on technology innovation and growth.
