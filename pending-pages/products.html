Citrus 65

Citrus 65 is Aventude's internal acceleration framework built especially for startups and product MVP development. It scaffolds both the frontend and backend, providing a ready-made foundation that helps teams move from concept to prototype with speed and confidence. Beyond new builds, Citrus 65 also enables engineers to understand and extend existing codebases faster, reducing onboarding and refactoring time. Its flexible design lets developers plug into any architecture — microservices, monoliths, or cloud-native — ensuring freedom without compromising structure. With Citrus 65, startups can focus on innovation while accelerating time to market.

QueryOn

QueryOn is an AI-powered conversational analytics tool developed by Aventude to make data access simple and intuitive. It allows customers to connect directly to their data sources and generate real-time insights through natural language queries — no complex dashboards or technical expertise required. By transforming everyday questions into meaningful reports and visualizations, QueryOn helps teams make faster, data-driven decisions and brings true self-service intelligence to business users.

SaaS Monetization Platform

Aventude's Business Enablement Platform helps SaaS and product companies scale faster by simplifying core business operations. It brings together payment gateways, metering and license management, and insightful dashboards that drive revenue and compliance, while AI-powered recommendations help optimize user conversion and engagement.

Built on React + .NET/Python with MongoDB or Table Streams, it can be deployed as a SaaS or self-hosted on Azure, offering full transparency through an RTL license. Secure by design, it handles licensing without touching sensitive user data.

With this platform, our customers can accelerate go-to-market, reduce development effort, and focus on scaling their products efficiently.

IntegrityAlert.ai

IntegrityAlert.ai is a corporate whistleblowing and incident reporting platform powered by AI. It leverages intelligent automation to understand, categorize, and route incidents to the right case handlers, ensuring timely and accurate resolution. The platform emphasizes data security, confidentiality, and compliance, fully aligning with the EU Whistleblowing Directive. By simplifying investigations and compliance reporting, IntegrityAlert.ai helps organizations foster transparency, accountability, and the highest standards of integrity.
